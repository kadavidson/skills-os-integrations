04 Four Capabilities Stacked
Data layer Coursera catalog
Skill taxonomy + learning content
The world's largest professional learning catalog, mapped to a structured skill taxonomy. Ground Truth surfaces the right content — already in the platform your company pays for.
Signal layer MCP connectors
Behavioral intelligence from real work
MCP data from Claude, GitHub, Figma, and other tools turns day-to-day work patterns into skill evidence — without surveys, self-assessments, or manager observation.

AI synthesizes all sources into a targeted learning plan — not a recommendation list, but a structured program with rationale, timeline, and measurable milestones.
